What is Computational thinking?

What should we do when we encounter a complex problem and don't know where to start? Computational thinking (CT) can be helpful in this case. CT is a strategy to deal with complex problems. By learning computational thinking, we can master how to analyze new information and deal with new problems. This way of thinking leads to improved problem-solving skills, and this skill can be used in any field.
